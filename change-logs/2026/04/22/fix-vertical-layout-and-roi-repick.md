Fixed the portrait-phone HUD where the hero timer overflowed the viewport (old `clamp(160px, 40vh, 520px)` was height-driven; 5 tabular-serif digits ended up ~2.75× wider than the screen on 360–480 CSS-px phones) and the ROI thumbnail at top-right covered the number. Timer now uses `clamp(96px, min(18vw, 40vh), 360px)` with `max-width: calc(100vw - 32px)`, and the thumbnail anchors bottom-left per AGENTS.md §Session mode. Replaced the two-tap corner picker with "zoom-as-ROI": the visible viewport after pinch/pan IS the ROI. Fixes the race where the first finger of a pinch gesture registered as a stray corner tap, reduces setup to one button press, and makes re-framing mid-session a single tap on the ROI thumbnail.
