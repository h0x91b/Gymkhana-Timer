Re-acquire the screen Wake Lock when the page becomes visible again. The browser auto-releases the lock on tab switch / phone lock / incoming call, and previously the screen would resume its normal sleep timeout after the first interruption. A `visibilitychange` listener now retries `navigator.wakeLock.request('screen')` whenever the page returns to visible, guarded by a `wakeLockDesired` flag so we don't grab a lock before the camera has ever been started.
